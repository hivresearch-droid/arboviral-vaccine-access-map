# Methods

Data on vector presence, virus circulation, and access to licensed vaccines were compiled using a hierarchical evidence framework prioritising authoritative public-health sources.

Primary data sources included WHO Disease Outbreak News, Weekly Epidemiological Record, ECDC, PAHO, and CDC datasets, supplemented by national public-health surveillance portals.

Vector distribution was defined using published global occurrence datasets and ECDC/CDC monitoring data.

Virus circulation was categorised as endemic, sporadic, or outbreak-associated based on the consistency and recency of reported transmission.

Vaccine access was defined as regulatory approval or inclusion in national immunisation programmes (as of February 2026).

All classifications were cross-checked across multiple sources and assigned conservatively.
